Theproject

A new planetary book project entitled A Poem Between Us initiated by the Slovene artist Ištvan Išt Huzjan involves over 200 libraries and national book depositories from 200 World countries on 6 continents. For the project the artist is composing a unique book collection. It will contain a 1st book of poetry published in 2021 written in the national language or one of country’s official languages of every country of the World.

This collection will serve as a time capsule as a symbol of human connection through culture and beauty as a planetary poem in the form of a sculpture.

It is planned to be exhibited for the first time in September 2021 in Mexico City at the contemporary art gallery Proyectos Monclova.

The author

Ištvan Išt Huzjan was born on June 1st 1981 in Ljubljana, Slovenia. In 2005 he graduated from the Accademia di belle arti di Venezia in Italy. He registered his own book-publishing house 21st Century Books in 2006. In 2009 he finished the artist in residency program Rijksakademie van beeldende kunsten in Amsterdam and finished several other residencies between 2010 and 2020, amongst them the National Art Studio in Changdong in Seoul and Apartment of the Ministry of Culture of Slovenia in New York. In 2010 he cofounded the artist collective Project Goleb in Amsterdam. In 2014 he was awarded the Fernand Baudin prijs for most beautiful book of the year in Belgium and in 2015 he was awarded the Grand Prize of the 31. Biennial of Graphic Arts - Over You You at MGLC in Ljubljana. His more important recent solo exhibitions include De Metrico a Imperial, Proyectos Monclova, Mexico City, MX, overview exhibition MERE at City galleries and Museums of Ljubljana, SI and From Top To Bottom at Zbirka Vjenceslava Richtera i Nade Kareš / MSU Zagreb, Zagreb, HR. His more important recent group exhibitions include Happening: 20 years S.M.A.K., S.M.A.K., Gent, BE and Dead or Alive 9th Triennial of Contemporary Art - U3 at MG+MSUM in Ljubljana.

In 2012 he joined the Simplon Express / The Return a group exhibition set in a train traveling from Zagreb to Paris and wrote a book OHO and The Korean Avant-Garde Association where he compared the two for him seminal conceptual movements. That year he also traveled from Seoul to Ljubljana by land using only public transport as an artistic performance.

Since 2012 he is running an exhibition space in Brussels called Coffre Fort together with Gregoire Motte and Thibaut Espiau as the Artists Club.

Ištvan Išt Huzjan lives and works in Ljubljana, Slovenia.

The venue

Established in 2005, Proyectos Monclova is a leading contemporary art gallery based in Mexico City.

Representing a roster of some of Mexico’s most acclaimed contemporary artists and young talents, in 2015 the gallery sought to bolster its gallery programming by bringing in emerging and established international artists, further enriching their dialogue.

Proyectos Monclova has become a vital force within the Mexican art world, playing a critical role in expanding Mexico’s participation on the global arena while participating in numerous prestigious international art fairs.

The gallery continues to advance its mission by offering a dynamic program that seeks to enrich the local and international discourse through notable exhibitions, institutional collaborations, artist initiatives and scholarly publications.
